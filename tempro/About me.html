About me

Hello! I'm Federico Bolletta, a 19 years old graphic designer from Milan. Currently, I am pursuing my studies at the Politecnico di Milano, where I am honing my skills in both digital and analog art.From a young age, I've been fascinated by the power of visual storytelling. My passion for art led me to explore various mediums, and I discovered a love for combining traditional techniques with modern technology. This unique approach allows me to create designs that are both timeless and contemporary.
